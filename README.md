KOSH – Gen Z Money OS
KOSH is an AI-powered financial literacy app for Indian youth (13–25). It tracks expenses, builds savings habits, and teaches money in fun, simple language.
Demo flow (MVP)


Riya (17) signs up with her phone number and selects “School student”.


She sees her dashboard with last week’s expenses (some are auto-filled from mocked UPI data).


She creates a goal for a new phone and starts a daily savings plan.


The AI Money Mentor explains how her spending affects this goal and gives tips in Hinglish.


She unlocks a streak badge for logging in 3 days in a row.
